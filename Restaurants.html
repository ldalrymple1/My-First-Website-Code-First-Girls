Restaurants in Paris

Le Georges

Located at the top of the Pompidou Centre, this rooftop restaurant boasts stunning 360 degree views of Paris. Enjoy a refreshing cocktail or treat yourself to dinner- don't miss out on this one!

Last updated 3 mins ago

La Marine

Find this cosy traditional French Bistrot on the banks on the Canal Saint-Martin. At its best on a Sunday afternoon, treat yourself to Coq au vin or Steak haché with a glass of the house red wine.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Chez Julien

With an ongoing rivalry with its neighbour Café Louis-Phillippe this restaurant is always trying to outdo itself, and yes it succeeds! Quick service and mouth wateringly good food.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Bistrot Vivienne

Just a few streets away from the Louvre, this is the perfect place to stop for lunch to break up your day of sightseeing. Arguably the best bistrot in Paris!

Last updated 3 mins ago

Ober Mamma

If you love pizza, this is the place for you! They don't take reservations so sometimes you have to queue, but we assure you its well worth the wait! Our favourite dish has to be the famous truffle pasta or the prosciutto pizza.

Last updated 3 mins ago

Au Rocher du Cancale

Located on the busy bustling street of Rue Montorgeuil in teh cneter of Paris, this beautiful restaurant is the perfect spot for people watching. Better for a café and a croissant rather than lunch but the food is still good!
