Bars in Paris

Here are a selection of our favourite bars in Paris.
~ Santé!

La Mezcalaría

Located at the top of the Pompidou Centre, this rooftop restaurant boasts stunning 360 degree views of Paris. Enjoy a refreshing cocktail or treat yourself to dinner- don't miss out on this one!

La Gare

Find this cosy traditional French Bistrot on the banks on the Canal Saint-Martin. At its best on a Sunday afternoon, treat yourself to Coq au vin or Steak haché with a glass of the house red wine.

Le Perchoir

With an ongoing rivalry with its neighbour Café Louis-Phillippe this restaurant is always trying to outdo itself, and yes it succeeds! Quick service and mouth wateringly good food.
