Bars in Paris

Here are a selection of our favourite bars in Paris.
~ Santé!

La Mezcalaría

One of Paris' most hipster speak eazys. It is hidden behind the kitchen of a Peruvian restaurant. Mezcal is very on trend at the moment and this is the place to go to drink it in Paris.Très à la mode!

La Gare

Listen to jazz in an abandoned train station. Free concerts everyday from 9pm and affordable drinks from the bar: cocktails are around €7 and pints start at €5

Le Perchoir

This huge rooftop bar has a 360-degree view of the capital. Littered with comfy sofas with wacky cushions, colourful flowers and hanging plants. Best time to go: sunset!

Le Pavillon des Canaux

Set in a house that overlooks the Canal de l’Ourq, this edgy café-restaurant also has its own colourfully painted houseboats. Come and enjoy the community vibe on a warm sunny day and sit outside at a table by the water.

Le Sully

In the heart of the 10e on rue Faubourg Saint-Denis, Le Sully is an incredibly popular institution. And with good reason. Make it your watering hole with just €3.50 pints and €3 shots! Expect boho people boozing and the constant clinking of glasses.

La Recyclerie

Located in an old abandoned railway station this bar and restaurant also has a vegetable garden and its own daily DIY workshops. A very chilled out and relaxed welcoming vibe. Combine with a trip to the flea market next door!
